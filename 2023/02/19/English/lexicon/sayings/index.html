做人

为人处事

Honesty is the best policy.
诚实不欺为上策。

Respect yourself, or no one else willrespect you.
要人尊敬，必须自重。

He is not fit to command others thatcannot command himself.
正人须先正己。

Do as you would be done by.
己所不欲，勿施于人。

One good turn deserves another.
行善积德。

Good is rewarded with good.
善有善报。

A candle lights others and consumesitself.
蜡烛照亮别人，却毁灭了自己。

Patience is the best remedy.
忍耐是良药。

Pride goes before, and shame comesafter.
骄傲使人落后。

He knows most who speaks least.
大智若愚。

A still tongue makes a wise head.
寡言者智。

Please the eye and plague the heart.
贪图一时快活，必然留下隐祸。

Penny wise, pound foolish.
贪小便宜吃大亏。

The farthest way about is the nearestway home.
抄近路反而绕远路。

Take things as they come.
既来之，则安之。

He who makes constant complaint getslittle compassion.
经常诉苦，没人同情。

When in Rome, do as the Romans do.
入乡随俗。

Great minds think alike. 
英雄所见略同。 

Don’t troubles trouble until trouble troubles you. 
不要自找麻烦。 

Don’t try to teach your grandmother to suck eggs.
不要班门弄斧。

友谊

Birds of a feather flock together.
物以类聚，人以群分。

A man is known by his friends.
什么人交什么朋友。

People judge you by the company you keep.

Keep good men company and you shall beof the number.
近朱者赤，近墨者黑。

A friend in need is a friend indeed.
Misfortune tests the sincerity offriends.
患难见真情。

A friend is easier lost than found.
得朋友难，失朋友易。

A faithful friend is hard to find.
知音难觅；益友难得。

A friend is never known till a man has need.
需要之时方知友。

做事

坚持与成事

No way is impossible to courage.
勇者无惧。

Nothing is too difficult in the world if you set your mind into it. 
世上无难事，只怕有心人。 

Where there is a will, there is a way.
有志者，事竟成。 

Stick to it, and you’ll succeed.
只要人有恒，万事都能成。

Success belongs to the persevering.
坚持就是胜利。

The finest diamond must be cut.
玉不琢，不成器。

No rose without a thorn.
没有不带刺的玫瑰。

Rome is not built in a day.
罗马不是一天建成的。

A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step.
千里之行始于足下。 

One false step will make a great difference. 
失之毫厘，谬之千里。

More hasty，less speed. 
欲速则不达。 

Well begun is half done.
A good beginning is half done.
良好的开端是成功的一半。

All things are difficult before theyare easy.
凡事总是由难而易。

It is the first step that coststroublesome.
万事开头难。

After a storm comes a calm.
雨过天晴。 

Let’s cross the bridge when we come to it.
船到桥头自然直。

All roads lead to Rome.
条条大路通罗马。 

Man proposes, god disposes.
谋事在人，成事在天。

God helps those who help themselves. 
天助自助者。 

  • We must not lie down, and cry, “God help us.”
    求神不如求己。 

  • God helps those who help themselves.
    自助者，天助之。

 

Every man is the architect of his ownfortune.
自己的命运自己掌握。

No pains，no gains. 
一份耕耘，一份收获。

勤奋

The early bird catches the worm.
早起的鸟儿有虫吃。

Diligence is the mother of success.
Industry is the parent of success.
勤奋是成功之母。

Care and diligence bring luck.
谨慎和勤奋才能抓住机遇。

Genius is nothing but labor anddiligence.
天才不过是勤奋而已。

Genius is one percent inspiration and 99 percent perspiration.
天才一分来自灵感，九十九分来自勤奋。

做事

He who does not advance loses ground.
逆水行舟，不进则退。

A little of every thing is nothing in the main.
每事浅尝辄止，事事都告无成。

Strike the iron while it is hot.
趁热打铁。

Make hay while the sun shines.
良机勿失。

A bird in the hand is worth than two in the bush.
一鸟在手胜过双鸟在林；多得不如现得，有总比没有好。

Look before you leap.
摸清情况再行动。

Learn to walk before you run.
循序渐进。

A man can do no more than he can.
凡事都应量力而行。

Look before you leap. 
三思而后行。

Look before you leap. First think, then act.
三思而后行。 

No man is wise at all times.
智者千虑，必有一失。 

Experience must be bought.
吃一堑，长一智。

He laughs best who laughs last.
谁笑到最后，谁笑得最好。

It is never too late to mend.
亡羊补牢，犹为未晚。 

It is no use crying over spilt milk.
覆水难收。

惜时

Time flies.
光阴似箭。

Time has wings.
光阴去如飞。

Time lost cannot be won again.
时光一去不复返。

Time and tide wait for no man.
时不我待。

Time is money.
时间就是金钱。 

Lost years are worse than lost dollars.
失去时光金不换。

To save time is to lengthen life.
节约时间就是延长生命。

Time stays not the fool’s leisure.
时间不等闲逛的傻瓜。

A plant may produce new flowers; man is young but once.
花有重开日，人无再少年。 

Art is long, but life is short.
艺术千秋，人生朝露；吾生也有涯，而知也无涯；艺术是漫长的，但生命是短暂的。

  • Where there is life, there is hope.
    生命不息，希望常在。

 

An idle youth，a needy age. 
少壮不努力，老大徒伤悲。

What may be done at any time will be done at no time.
明日待明日，明日不再来。 

Never put off till tomorrow what you can do today.
Don’t put off till tomorrow what shouldbe done today.
今天能做的事绝不要拖到明天；今日事，今日毕。

学习

Readingis to the mind while exercise to the body.
读书健脑，运动强身。

It’s never too old to learn. 
活到老，学到老。

Learn and live.
活着，为了学习。

There is no royal road to learning.
书山有路勤为径，学海无涯苦作舟。

There is no royal road to learning.
学无坦途。 

Practice makes perfect. 
熟能生巧。 

Constant dripping wears away a stone.
水滴石穿，绳锯木断。

Complacency is the enemy of study.
学习的敌人是自己的满足。

Knowledge makes humble; ignorance makesproud.
知识使人谦虚，无知使人傲慢。

Knowledge advances by steps and not byleaps.
知识只能循序渐进，不能跃进。

A man cannot spin and reel at the sametime.
一心不能二用。

A man becomes learned by asking questions.
不耻下问才能有学问。

读书

Books, like friends, should be few and well chosen.
读书如择友，宜少且宜精。 

A book that remains shut is but a block.
书本不常翻，犹如一块砖。

道理

规律与哲理

Every coin has two sides. 
每枚硬币都有两面；凡事皆有好坏。 

Every advantage has its disadvantage.
有利必有弊。

Every man has his faults.
金无足赤，人无完人。

Adversity makes a man wise, not rich.
逆境出人才。

（待补）
重赏之下，必有勇夫。

Great hopes make great man.
远大的抱负，造就伟大的人物。

Great men’s sons seldom do well.
富不过三代。

Great trees are good for nothing but shade.
大树底下好乘凉。

Great wits have short memories.
贵人多忘事。

Light come, light go.
来得容易，去得快。 

Every day is not Sunday.
好景不常在。

Habit is a second nature.
习惯成自然。

Still water run deep.
静水流深。

No matter how high the mountain is, its name will spread far and wide if there is a fairy.
山不在高，有仙则名。

Lookers-on see most of the game.
旁观者清，当局者迷。

Two heads are better than one.
三个臭皮匠顶个诸葛亮。 

Four eyes see more than two.
集思广益。

Knowledge starts with practice.
实践出真知。

Easier said than done. 
说起来容易，做起来难。

Action speaks louder than words.
Fact speak louder than words.
Facts speak plainer than words. 
事实胜于雄辩；行胜于言。 

Seeing is believing.
眼见为实；百闻不如一见。

All that glitters is not gold.
闪光的不一定都是金子。

健康

An apple a day keeps the doctor away.
一天一苹果，医生远离我。

Prevention is better than cure.
预防胜于治疗。

Good health is over wealth.
健康是最大的财富。

Health is not valued till sicknesscomes.
病时方知健康可贵。

Happiness lies first of all in health.
幸福首先在于健康。

A good medicine tastes bitter.
良药苦口。

A close mouth catches no flies.
病从口入。

All work and no play makes Jack a dullboy.
只工作，不玩耍，聪明孩子也变傻。 

Cheerfulness is health; its opposite,melancholy, is disease.
欢乐就是健康，忧郁就是病痛。

Content is better than riches.
知足者常乐。

He is happy that thinks himself so.
自乐者常乐。

钱财

Gold will not buy anything.
黄金不能买尽一切。

Wealth makes worship.
财富能使人拜倒。

The chief aim of man is not to get money.
人的主要目的并不是赚钱。

The money the miser hoards will do himno good.
守财奴积财，对自己毫无好处。

What is wealth good for, if it bringsmelancholy?
财富如带忧郁来，有了财富有何用？

自然

Kill the goose and lays the golden eggs.
杀鹅取卵；竭泽而渔。

A timely snow promises a good harvest.
瑞雪兆丰年。
