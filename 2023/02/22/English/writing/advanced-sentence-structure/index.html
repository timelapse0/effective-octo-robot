高级句式

成分划分

v

entitle

show/express –> carry

? –> shape

witness

let –> enable
discourage sb from doing sth.

believe –> be convinced of/that

come as
act as
serve as
come with

seem to be
appear to be
be proved to be
serve to do with

serve a …… funtion for doing sth.

eg. Pain comes as a natural defence reaction.

n

people –> folks

food –> food supply
adv. –> on a adj. basis

advantages –> the outstanding points

the government –> the authorities concerned

pron

such + pron. 这样……

This is –> Such is……

You can contact ==whoever== it in charge.

adj

helpful –> be of some help

undisputable 无可争辩的；无可置疑的（incapable of being questioned or disputed）

dispute: to engage in argument，especially : to argue irritably or with irritating persistence

of course/sure –> certainly

more/most –> adj./adv. than ever before

a good choice –> a preferable/appropriate/excellent option

adv

not necessarily 不一定
in fact –> as a matter of fact
finally –> ultimately

prep

for example → A wonderful example is …

phrase

as per 根据 = according to
Given 考虑到；鉴于

to put this into perspective
就这一点而言

To put in a nutshell
To draw a conclusion
总之

  • 表达个人观点
    In my view/opinion
    In my eyes
    as far as I know
    As far as I see it
    As far as I am concerned
    from my viewpoint/standpoint
    from one’s perspective

From my point of view

As per my perspective
Personally
Personally/Frankly speaking

  • 引用

As the saying/proverb/a classic(经典)/ says/goes/runs, …

https://forum.wordreference.com/threads/as-the-proberb-runs-goes-says.676179/

It going without saying that … 不言而喻

There is much truth in the saying that 谚语……说的很有道理

As every knows, ……

it seems to me that…

Sentence

  • It 句型

it is convincing that……

it is apparent that…

it is natural that… ……很正常
it is understandable that… ……可以理解

It can be safely said that…… 可以有把握地说……

it is universally acknowledged that…
……是被广泛承认的。
It is generally recognized that……
人们普遍认为……

  • There 句型
    There is much evidence that… 不少证据表明……

There is much truth in the idea (that…) ……这种观点是有道理的。

  • S. find it/sth. ……

  • S. leave O. ……

  • 倒装句

  • 比较级
    Children usually have far more potential than their parents had realized.
    孩子们都有很大的潜能，而父母却没有意识到这一点。

  • 讲故事
    I never forget in mind the day that…
    There was a time when I …
    Once upon a time sb. was just a …

运用划分

advantages

….. enjoy considerable advantages

benefits

Working out in the morning provides addtional benefits beyonds being physically fit.

methods

What we learn from the concept of …… is that we are best equipped to realize the positive true nature we each have when we are emotionally honest and flexible.

disadvantages

However, we should look at the other side of the coin.

Suggestion

I’ d like to give/share three pieces of advice hoped to be helpful to you.

descriptions

…… opens up access to ……

…… go beyond expectation in terms of doing sth.

eg. They went beyond expectations in terms of really the situation and learning what my specific needs were.

The same can be true for ……
The same thing applies to ……

  • 相似
    … bears startling /striking resemblance to… .
    …与….极为相似
comparison

these advantages pale when compared with……

argument

And the opposite is also the case.
反之亦然。

补充

Confucianism

Confucianism is more like a way to look at the world,

it pushes people to have self-discipline and a love for humanity.

Flexible Sentence Structure 复杂句型

Through the application of great effort over many years, ……

If you……, you will definitely see the outcome.

If everyone followed a similar plan, the results would be impressive.
