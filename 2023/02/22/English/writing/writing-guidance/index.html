要点

  • 行云流水——流畅 smooth：cohesion coherence

方法参考

  1. 替代、拓长

  • 用一个词组替代单个单词
    think > hold the view that
    attention > attention span: the length of time during which one (such as an individual or a group) is able to concentrate or remain interested

  • 用名词短语代替动词
    have a good command of

  • 带名词的介词短语
    with the purpose of

  1. 充实
  • 用高级的形容词丰富语句，用副词修饰形容词
    I am exceedingly delighted to/that
  1. 运用固定搭配

  • 运用固定词组
    Let’s make our joint effort to xx
    give access to a wider range of …
    as scheduled
    be available for

  • 较高级的 v. + n. 结构
    appreciate your attendance

  • 使用形式主语（It is …… that ……）和形式宾语（v. + it）
    make it possible

  1. 使用更复杂而地道的表达

  • 否定表示肯定
    spare no effort
    There/It is no doubt that

  • 被动表示主动

  1. 顺畅
  • 使用高级连接词、过渡句
