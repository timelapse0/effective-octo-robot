Tutoring or mentoring

Tutoring is the act or process of conveying knowledge or skills to another. Mentoring is a synonym of tutoring. Mentoring involves informal communication, usually face-to-face and during a sustained period of time, between a person who is perceived to have greater relevant knowledge, wisdom, or experience (the mentor) and a person who is perceived to have less (the protégé (门徒)).

moreover，what’s more

都有“此外，而且”的含义。

moreover：前面所说的及后面所补充的是并列关系。
例子：Moreover, the researchers discovered that these two connections were independent of each other.此外，研究人员发现，这两个联系是彼此独立存在的。

what’s more ：含递进含义。
例子：What’s more, obstructive sleep apnea has been shown to affect the cognitive abilities and behavior of children with the condition.
不仅如此，有证据表明，阻塞性睡眠呼吸暂停综合征会影响儿童的认知能力和行为方式。

介词

一、年、月、季节、周用in

二、具体日用on

三、

1 一般以day结尾的节日名称前用介词on

2 一般以festival结尾的节日名称前用介词in

3 圣诞节、新年、复活节和感恩节，指整个节日用at，指节日中的某一天用on，强调是在整个期间用during。
